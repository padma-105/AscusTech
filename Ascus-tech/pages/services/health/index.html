Ascus Tech : Our Health Care Service

At Ascus Tech, we specialize in delivering cutting-edge software solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of businesses across various industries. Headquartered in Hyderabad, India, we combine technical expertise with industry insights to drive digital transformation and empower organizations to thrive in today’s dynamic business landscape.

Claims Submission:

AscusTech facilitates timely and accurate claims submission to insurance payers, ensuring prompt reimbursement for healthcare services rendered. Our billing experts meticulously review claims for accuracy and compliance with payer guidelines, minimizing the risk of claim denials and rejections.

Revenue Cycle Optimization:

We help healthcare organizations optimize their revenue cycle from patient registration to claims adjudication. Our comprehensive approach includes patient eligibility verification, charge capture, coding, claims submission, payment posting, and denial ma nagement, ensuring smooth and efficient revenue flow.

Accounts Receivable Management:

AscusTech offers accounts receivable management services to track, monitor, and follow up on outstanding claims and unpaid balances. Our billing specialists work diligently to identify and resolve claim denials, appeals, and underpayments, maximizing revenue collection and minimizing revenue leakage.

Denial Management:

We assist healthcare organizations in effectively managing claim denials and appeals to recover lost revenue and improve cash flow. Our team analyzes denial trends, identifies root causes, and implements corrective actions to prevent future denials and op timize reimbursement rates..

Patient Billing and Collections:

AscusTech provides patient billing and collections services to streamline the billing process and improve patient satisfaction. We generate clear and easy -to-understand patient statements, offer convenient payment options, and follow up with patients to ensure prompt payment of outstanding balances.
