Hiring for Business Development Manager

Job Title: Business Development Manager

Company: Ascustech Software

Location: Hyderabad, India

About Us

Ascustech Software is a renowned software development company based in Hyderabad, recognized for its innovative solutions and commitment to excellence. With a focus on delivering cutting-edge software products across various industries, we pride ourselves on our talented team and collaborative work environment.

Position Overview

We are seeking a dynamic and results-driven Business Development Manager to join our team and drive business growth. The Business Development Manager will be responsible for identifying new business opportunities, building relationships with potential clients, and expanding our client base.

Responsibilities

  • Identify and research potential clients and business opportunities to generate new leads.
  • Build and maintain strong relationships with prospective clients through effective communication and networking.
  • Develop and implement strategic business development plans to achieve sales targets.
  • Collaborate with internal teams to develop tailored solutions for clients.
  • Prepare and deliver presentations, proposals, and pitches to clients.
  • Negotiate and finalize contracts and agreements.
  • Monitor market trends and competitor activities.
  • Provide regular updates and sales reports to senior management.

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Marketing, or a related field; Master’s preferred.
  • Experience in business development, sales, or account management, ideally in software.
  • Strong understanding of lead generation, prospecting, and closing deals.
  • Excellent communication, negotiation, and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to build and maintain client relationships at all levels.
  • Strategic thinking with strong drive to achieve results.
  • Motivated self-starter passionate about exceeding targets.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary package
  • Performance-based incentives
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Collaborative work environment

How to Apply

Submit your resume and cover letter to [email address]. Please include “Business Development Manager Application” in the subject line.

Join our team and be part of our exciting journey! Ascustech Software is an equal opportunity employer.
