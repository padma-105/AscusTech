Job Title: HR Manager

Company: Ascustech Software

Location: Hyderabad, India

About Us

Ascustech Software is a leading software development company based in Hyderabad, known for its innovative solutions and commitment to excellence. We specialize in developing cutting-edge software products for various industries, and our talented team is at the heart of our success.

Position Overview

We are seeking an experienced and dynamic HR Manager to join our team and lead our human resources department. The HR Manager will be responsible for developing and implementing HR strategies and initiatives that align with the company’s goals and objectives. This is an excellent opportunity for an HR professional looking to make a significant impact in a fast-paced and collaborative environment.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement HR policies and procedures in line with the company’s objectives.
  • Oversee the recruitment and onboarding process.
  • Manage employee relations and support both staff and management.
  • Administer benefits and ensure compliance with regulations.
  • Coordinate performance reviews and goal setting.
  • Lead training and development programs.
  • Maintain accurate HR records and ensure data protection compliance.
  • Stay updated with HR trends and best practices.

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in HR, Business Administration, or related field; Master’s preferred.
  • Experience as an HR Manager or similar role in the software industry.
  • Strong knowledge of HR laws and practices.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities and deliver results.
  • High integrity and professionalism.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary package
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Flexible work hours
  • Career advancement opportunities

How to Apply

Submit your resume and cover letter to [email address]. Please include “HR Manager Application” in the subject line.

Join our team and be a part of our exciting journey! Ascustech Software is an equal opportunity employer.
